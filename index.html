Welcome to Excellence

A Golden Sanctuary in Hawassa

Located on the tranquil shores of Lake Hawassa, Golden Gate Hotel combines modern architectural mastery with the warm hospitality of Ethiopia. Our mission is to provide an oasis of calm and luxury for the discerning traveler.

From our artisan-crafted interiors to our world-class dining, every detail is designed to evoke a sense of golden refinement.
